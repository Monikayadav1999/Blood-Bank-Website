1) How frequently can I donate Blood?

Males - Every 3 months Females - Every 4 months

2) How can I find blood availability in this website?

We have an option called "Find your Blood Group" at Home page. Click on the link.

  • Select your District from the list.
  • Select your nearest Blood Bank from the list of the concerned district.
  • Select the blood Group you are searching for.
  • Click on the search button.

3) What can I do before and after giving blood?

Be sure to eat at your regular mealtimes and drink plenty of fluids before and after donating, but avoid alcohol.

4) Will I feel all right after donation?

Yes, you will feel all right after donation.
